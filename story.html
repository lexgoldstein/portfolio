San Francisco's new mayor brands fentanyl state of emergency

By Lex Goldstein

Levi Strauss heir Daniel Lurie swore into office on Jan. 8 with a mayoral promise to begin addressing San Francisco's fentanyl crisis on day one of his term.

Lurie will head projects to create a permanent Drug Market Agency Coordination Center and a 24/7 Drop-Off Crisis Stabilization Unit, mainly for police use.
